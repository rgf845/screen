## Features That Actually Matter

**Automatic Zoom That Just Works**

Screen Studio watches where you click and automatically zooms in. No manual work, no timeline fiddling. Your demos become instantly more engaging, especially for viewers on smaller screens. You can also manually select areas to zoom if you want more control.

**Smooth Cursor Movement**

Remember those shaky, rapid cursor movements that make viewers dizzy? Screen Studio transforms them into smooth, professional glides. It's like the difference between handheld camera footage and a stabilized gimbal shot.

**4K Recording Quality**

Your recordings come out crisp in up to 4K at 60fps. Text stays readable, UI details remain sharp, and you can export to pretty much any format you need - MP4, MOV, or even highly optimized GIFs.

**iPhone & iPad Recording**

Connect your iOS device via USB cable and Screen Studio automatically detects the model and color. Perfect for app demos where you need to show both desktop and mobile interfaces. No third-party software needed.

**Audio Enhancement**

Voice volume gets normalized automatically, background noise disappears, and you can record system audio from specific apps. Your neighbor's lawnmower won't ruin your tutorial anymore.

**AI-Powered Subtitles**

Generate transcripts and add them as subtitles - all processed locally on your machine. No data sent to external servers, which is refreshing in 2026.

**One-Click Export Presets**

Choose your platform - YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, whatever - and Screen Studio optimizes the export settings automatically. Switch between 16:9 widescreen, 9:16 vertical, 1:1 square, you name it.
